Shipping and payment

We will use the address provided to write a notification to the blockchain when shipped. After use, all data will be destroyed.
1) Buy iHodL: send 0.05 bitcoin to this address:
35zLZS9Lehw3x3Vo89M5QwTbSr5WfV54iP
2) Wait for Christmas
3) Take your iHodL to a safe place, without camera or people around
4) Connect the device to a power outlet and wait for keys to appear (do not connect to a computer), stay off line.
5) Hard copy your keys (best way: engrave your keys on metal or write them on a sheet of paper, wood…)
6) Put your private key in a safe place (safe, bank, garden,…)
7) Transfer your bitcoins (from coinbase, Binance,…) with your public key.
8) Repeat this operation (from 4 to 8) as many times as you want new keys. You can erase the keys (that you hard copied) from the screen by disconnecting and connecting again the Ihodl.
To put your Bitcoins again online, use existing private key swiping software.
